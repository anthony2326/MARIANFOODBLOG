KINILAW NA ISDA(RAW FISH)

INGREDIENTS

• FISH

• SALT

• VINEGAR

• TOMATO

• GINGER

• LEMON

• ONION

• CHILL

Procedure for making Kinilaw na Isda (Raw Fish)

1. Gather fresh fish (usually firm and white-fleshed), vinegar (preferably cane vinegar), onions, ginger, chili peppers, calamansi or lime, and salt.

2. Clean the fish thoroughly, removing scales, guts, and bones. You can cut the fish into bite-sized cubes or thin slices, depending on your preference.

3. In a bowl, combine the fish with vinegar, ensuring it is fully submerged. Let it marinate for about 30 minutes to an hour. The acidity of the vinegar "cooks" the fish.

4. After marinating, drain excess vinegar from the fish. Add chopped onions, ginger, chili peppers.
